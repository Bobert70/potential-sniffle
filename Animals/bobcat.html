Bobcat

Bobcats are found in both the upper and northern lower peninsulas of Michigan. Make sure to check the trapping rules on the michigan website, as the limits are very specific concerning how and how many bobacts you can trap per season. You can use footholds and some other weird cage trap that is NOT covered here. Any other trap is illegal to use. Still check the rules, though. Bait includes dead animals, specifically beaver meat or small game, fresh or slightly aged. Fish works too.
