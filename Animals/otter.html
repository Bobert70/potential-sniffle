Otter

Otters (specifically river otters) are pretty common throughout Michigan. They are smallish mammels that live in water. They eat primarily fish, which they hunt. Good bait is fish. Live ones can work pretty well here, but as with any carnivorous hunter, make sure the fish is relatively fresh so the otter will eat. You can trap them with both conibears and footholds. There is a bag limit, so be sure to check the rules regarding otter in Michigan.
