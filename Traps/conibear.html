Conibear

Coninears are great at trapping numerous animals. They work by having a trigger that, when tripped, causes the the claws to snap shut, usually on the animals neck or upper torso area. These traps are primarily used to trap animals such as beaver, raccoon, skunk, mink, muskrat, otter, and opossums. Look for the 2026 Furbearer harvest regulations summary to find rules regarding these traps.
